7. Extended Database Models (Phase 2 & Beyond)

These physical tables support the advanced tracking and gamification mechanics of the application.

transfers
Columns: id, from_account_id, to_account_id, amount, transfer_date

recurring_transactions
Columns: id, frequency, next_run_date, template_json, auto_create

budgets
Columns: id, category_id, period_type, budget_amount

goals & goal_contributions
goals: id, title, target_amount, current_amount, target_date, status
goal_contributions: id, goal_id, transaction_id, amount

investments
Columns: id, account_id, investment_type, symbol, units, avg_cost, current_value

loans & emis
loans: id, lender_name, loan_type, principal_amount, interest_rate, outstanding_amount
emis: id, loan_id, emi_amount, due_date, status

subscriptions
Columns: id, name, amount, billing_cycle, next_renewal

gamification tables
achievements: id, code, title, xp_reward
user_achievements: id, achievement_id, unlocked_at
xp_events: id, source, xp_amount

wealth_garden_items: id, goal_id, tree_type, growth_stage

backups
Columns: id, drive_file_id, checksum, backup_date

8. Financial & Analytics Engines

Financial Engine Responsibilities: Handles account balance calculations, transfer processing, budget tracking, goal progress, net worth calculations, and cash flow.

Analytics Engine: Generates daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, and yearly summaries. Calculates metrics like income vs. expense, savings rate, category breakdown, investment growth, and net worth trends.

Smart Insights: Auto-generates anomaly detections (e.g., "Food spending increased 23%").

9. Gamification Engine

XP System: Rewards users for actions like logging transactions or staying under budget.

Financial Health Score: Calculated monthly based on savings rate, debt ratio, and budget adherence.

Wealth Garden: Visual representation where savings goals grow into trees as milestones are reached.

Boss Battle: Monthly financial reviews summarizing goals and budgets.

10. Backup, Sync, & Security

Offline First: All operations write locally to SQLite. UI updates immediately without network dependency.

Google Drive Backup: Database is exported, encrypted (AES-256), compressed, and uploaded to Drive.

Future Sync (Rust + Axum): Incremental sync using change logs (sync_events table) with last-write-wins conflict resolution.

Security: PIN lock, biometrics, encrypted database (SQLCipher), and secure key storage.
